Home

People will come to this site in search of an amazing photographer that wants to and is eager to capture their most precious memories in a beautiful and meaningful way. When they get to the site, they will be greeted with a lovely picture and introduction of the photographer, my grandma, after that, they will see a few examples of her work, and have the option to explore more rich content and edits, as well as look at pricing, so they can get a general idea of the cost and the affordability. From there off from the homepage, there will be a seamless and secure way to enter their information, and my grandma’s information will be inline, right there making accessibility effortless for the potential client.

Possible questions are probably going to be related to pricing and contact, which I have addressed with about me and contact me pages. Direct ease of access to book an appointment and immediately be on my grandma’s radar through a message will be a priority for the site. The site will also have a link to my grandma's Facebook so that the clients can get to know her on a more personal level. This will help engagement on the site and in her business. The seamless and intuitive does of the website is what is going to attract clients better then other sites. Apart from just looking beautiful, it will have an ease of use that the clients will appreciate. Growing interaction between my grandma’s Facebook, updated website, and happy clients is what’s going to give the site longevity in the market and keep people coming back for more. Word will spread and engagement will be high and the website is a key part of that and how it all begins There will definitely also have to be a page where sample content is shown along with pricing for the different types of sessions. Portraits, family photos, graduation photos, babies, engagements, and and more
